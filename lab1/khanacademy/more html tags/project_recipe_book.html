Naryn

  • Time: 4 h 30 m
  • Serves: 8
Ingredients Quantity
Lamb meat on the bone 1 kg
Kazy horse sausage 0.5 kg
Onion 3 onion
Flour 1 kg
Chicken egg 3 pcs
Peppercorns 10 pcs
Carnation 5 pcs
Sunflower Oil 3 spoons
Water tot broth 3 glasses
Water to dough Pods 1 glass

Step 1: Shake eggs with salt. Pour in water, stir. Pour in the flour. Knead elastic dough. Roll out the layer, cut into thin strips. The noodles will need to be cooked separately.

Step 2: Pour water into a pot or saucepan. Rinse the meat, cut into large pieces. Drop into water.

Step 3: Add whole onion, cloves, spices.

Step 4: Separately, we put kazy to cook. Remove the finished sausage from the pan, lower the noodles into the broth for three minutes. Then strain.

Step 5: Cut kazy into strips.

Step 6: Pour sunflower oil into a saucepan. Peel the onion, chop in the form of half rings. Fry in hot oil. Remove to a plate. Use oil to lubricate the noodles.

Step 7: Cool the prepared lamb a little, cut into thin strips, mix with sausage.

Step 8: Place all ingredients on a large serving plate. Wash your hands thoroughly with soap and mix everything with your hands, including the onion. Pour lamb broth into a bowl, put a plate with the rest of the ingredients.

Slow Cooker Flank Steak Fajitas

  • Time: 8 h
  • Serves: 8
Ingredients Quantity
Beef Flank Steak 1 steak (2 lbs)
Green Pepper 1 medium pepper, cut into strips
Sweet Red Pepper 1 medium pepper, cut into strips
Onion 1 medium onion, halved and sliced
Fajita Seasoning Mix 1 envelope
Beer 1/2 cup
Flour Tortillas 8 tortillas (warmed)
Pico de Gallo 1 cup

Step 1: Place first five ingredients in a 5-qt. slow cooker. Pour beer over top. Cook, covered, on low until meat is tender, 6-8 hours.

Step 2: Remove beef from slow cooker. Strain vegetable mixture; return vegetables to slow cooker. (Discard cooking juices or save for another use.) Shred beef with two forks; add to vegetables and heat through. Serve in tortillas with pico de gallo and cilantro.
